Status Tracking Map

The purpose of this page is to allow team members to visually track the progress of property environmental inspections by location. Locations are approximate based on Google geocoding of provided addresses. Entries having identical property addresses are displayed as a single map pin.

This data is maintained by Franklin Associates in a Google Fusion Table and may not be fully up-to-date with the main project tracking database in AirTable.
