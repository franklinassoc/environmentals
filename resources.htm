Process Overview

Environmental Service Request and Tracking Process

  1. LHC staff access the "New Request Form" page of this website to enter each inspection request; clicks Submit.
  2. A confirmation email is sent to staff person submitting the request and to Franklin Associates, alerting them to the request.
  3. System generates a summary sheet, deposits it into the Box.com "1. Incoming LHC Requests - INTAKE" folder by way of Google Drive.
  4. LHC staff access this Box.com INTAKE folder and upload supporting documents for each property
  5. Franklin Associates creates a unique Case # subfolder within Box.com for each property; moves case-specific files into each accordingly.
  6. Franklin staff screen requests for completeness; requesting supplemental information from LHC as required.
  7. When satisfactory, Franklin staff moves Case # folder into the Box.com "2. Franklin Approved Requests - INTAKE" folder. Here it is picked up by CSRS staff.
  8. CSRS staff review the case documents and forward to their environmental consultant PSI for investigation and fieldwork. These case # folders are moved by CSRS  to the folder named "3. CSRS Assignments In Progress" within Box.com.
  9. Fieldwork is now ongoing. Parties from PSI, CSRS or LHC may augment each Case # folder during the environmental inspection process. Monitoring and status updates can be accommodated at the project Dashboard, or directly at the AirTable database for editors with login credentials.
  10. As PSI completes cases, they will move the Box.com Case # subfolder into "4. CSRS Completed Tasks". Here they will receive a final quality review from CSRS and by Franklin.
  11. Completed cases will be submitted to LHC by Franklin Associates for review, and hopeful approval.
  12. Once each case has been accepted by LHC and approved, Franklin will move the Case # subfolder into "Z-Archive". Periodically, approved completed cases will be backed up and removed from the Box.com cloud environment.
