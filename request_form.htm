New Request Form

The purpose of this page is to allow LHC staff to enter a new request for property environmental inspection. When you click "submit", a confirmation email wil be sent to the named "requested by" staff person. In addition to submitting this form information, you will also need to upload supporting documents for each property into the project's Box.com INTAKE folder. Franklin staff will review, request additional information if necessary, and when complete will advance the property inspection case to CSRS for action.

Revisions

If you need to append or change information on a submitted request, this link to AirTables may be used to access the data table. Click the "LHC Requests tab, and then choose "Editing Grid" view directly beneath it. In this spreadsheet view you may notice that several fields (columns) are hidden. Most of these are filled in automatically by formula and therefore should not be edited directly.
